Rhiannon White

Director / Production Manager / Workshop Leader

Rhiannon co-founded Common Wealth, researched and created Our Glass House, The Ups and Downs of the Town of Brown and The Juniper Tree. Rhiannon has worked extensively with Circus 2 Palestine and has toured in both the West Bank and Gaza, using performance as a tool to promote wellbeing in often-desperate situations. Back in the UK she has run workshops with the Red Cross encouraging young people to take action on migration and development issues through performance. She also teaches circus and theatre to disadvantaged children with Bristol based Imayla. She has performed with the Invisible Circus, Glastonbury Festival and at Experimentica festival, Cardiff.
