Coco Banks

Set Designer / Devisor

Coco designs and performs with Common Wealth and stays up all night building sets. Coco facilitates community art workshops throughout wider London producing massive sculptures out of recycled and reclaimed materials and works in collaboration with other artists on refugee youth arts and theatre projects. Coco also builds sets and makes costume for House of Doll and The Invisible Circus.

Michelle Wren

Set Designer / Workshop Leader

Cardboard Club founder and self-taught carpenter, Michelle builds sets for Common Wealth Theatre, Invisible Circus, Bestival and other festivals. She also works as an artist facilitator working with people with special needs to make art for the Expressions exhibition every year. Michelle has also exhibited her own artwork at FACT Liverpool, The ManMuseum, Liverpool and worked as Artist in Residence at Knowle West Media Centre, Bristol.
