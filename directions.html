No Guts, No Heart, No Glory

We are sick of it, being ignored
We are sick of it, being indoors
A site-specific performance event based on interviews with Muslim Female Boxers. With electronic sound, magic, visual design and cinematic lighting, ‘No Guts, No Heart, No Glory explores being young, fearless and doing the unexpected.
