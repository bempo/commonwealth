Evie Manning

Director / Producer / Workshop Leader

Evie co-founded Common Wealth, directed and produced Our Glass House, An Indecent Incident and The Ups and Downs of the Town of Brown and The Juniper Tree with The Wonder Club. In addition to her work with Common Wealth, Evie has also worked making theatre with Metis Arts, Hope Street Ltd, TATE Liverpool, Tara Arts, The Albany Theatre and Caravan Stage.
