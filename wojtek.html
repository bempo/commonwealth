Wojtek Rusin

Sound Designer/ Devisor

Wojtek is a conceptual sound artist interested in exploring the potential of misinterpretation of different musical traditions and the authenticity of folk music. His latest release, Animalia, used layers of language, folk instruments and electronica, to produce a pseudo educational resource. Wojtek creates sound installations for theatre, exhibitions and performance art and has exhibited throughout the UK and Europe. He also works as a video artist producing graphics and artwork for music videos and short/feature films.
